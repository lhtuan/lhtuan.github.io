Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Bebi account and all associated data directly from the app:

  1. Open the Bebi app and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap the Settings icon on the home screen.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
What gets deleted: Your account, baby profiles, all tracked events (feeding, sleep, diaper, growth), and any associated data will be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

You can also request account deletion by emailing us at tl.tek5392@gmail.com with the email address associated with your account.
