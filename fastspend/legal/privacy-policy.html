Privacy Policy

FastSpend (com.tltek.fastspend) · Last updated 2 June 2026 · Effective 2 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TLTek ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the FastSpend mobile application (the "App"). FastSpend is an expense-tracking app designed to be local-first: by default, the financial data you enter stays on your device. Some optional features described below involve sending data to cloud services.

Summary. Your expenses, categories, and budgets are stored on your device. We do not run advertising and do not use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs. If you choose to enable the optional Cloud Backup feature, your data is synced to your private Google Firebase account and is accessible only to you.

1. Who we are

The App is provided by TLTek. For any privacy question or request, contact us at tl.tek5392@gmail.com.

Data controller: Tuan Le, 209, 355 Street, Tan An Hoi Commune, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

2. Information we process

2.1 Data you create in the App (stored on your device)

  • Transactions — amounts, currency, category, optional notes, and date/time.
  • Categories — names, icons, colors, and ordering.
  • Budgets — monthly limits per category.
  • Preferences — currency, language, and similar settings.

By default this data is stored only in a local database on your device and is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

2.2 Account data (only if you enable Cloud Backup)

Cloud Backup requires you to sign in with Google (via Firebase Authentication). When you do, we process the account information Google provides:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL
  • A Firebase user identifier (UID)

2.3 Synced data (only if you enable Cloud Backup)

When Cloud Backup is on, the data in Section 2.1 (transactions, categories, and budgets) is copied to Google Cloud Firestore under your account, at the path users/{your-id}/…. Security rules restrict this data so that only your signed-in account can read or write it. We, as operators, do not access the contents of your synced records in the ordinary course of providing the service.

2.4 What we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers and no ads.
  • No third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • No microphone access. Voice entry is handled by Google Assistant; the App only receives the resulting text and never records or stores audio.
  • No payment-card or banking-login details.

4. How we use information

  • To provide core expense-tracking features on your device.
  • To authenticate you and back up / sync your data across your devices when you enable Cloud Backup.
  • To send budget and sync notifications you have enabled.
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

5. Service providers

When Cloud Backup is enabled, we rely on Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), provided by Google LLC, to authenticate you and store your synced data. Google processes this data on our behalf and under its own terms. See the Firebase Privacy and Security information and the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App.
  • Synced data remains in Firestore until you delete it; deletions you make are propagated to the cloud and to your other devices on the next sync.
  • Signing out stops syncing and keeps your local data on the device.
  • To request deletion of your account and any associated cloud data, email tl.tek5392@gmail.com.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the GDPR or similar laws), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can:

  • Access and edit your data directly in the App.
  • Export your transactions to a CSV file using the in-App export feature.
  • Delete data in the App, or request deletion by contacting us.

To exercise any right, contact tl.tek5392@gmail.com.

8. Data security

Synced data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored within Google's infrastructure, protected by access rules that limit each record to its owner. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. International transfers

If you enable Cloud Backup, your data may be processed and stored by Google in data centers located outside your country of residence. Where required, such transfers are carried out under appropriate safeguards.

10. Children's privacy

FastSpend is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this policy or your data: tl.tek5392@gmail.com.
