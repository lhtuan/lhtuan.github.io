Delete your data

FastSpend (com.tltek.fastspend) · Last updated 13 July 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your FastSpend account and the data associated with it, what gets deleted, and what we keep. FastSpend is designed to be local-first: unless you sign in and turn on Cloud Backup, your data never leaves your device (see our Privacy Policy).

In short. If you signed in, the fastest way to delete everything from the cloud is in the app: Settings → Cloud Backup → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all synced data. Prefer not to use the app? Email tl.tek5392@gmail.com and we'll delete it for you.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

You need to be signed in to delete your account. In the FastSpend app:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to the Cloud Backup section.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Read the confirmation, then tap Delete.

Your account and all of its cloud data are deleted immediately, and you are signed out.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app (for example, you've uninstalled it), email tl.tek5392@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign in, with the subject "Delete my account". We may ask you to confirm ownership of the account before we proceed. We will complete the deletion within 30 days of verifying your request, and usually much sooner.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, from our cloud systems:

  • Your sign-in account (Firebase Authentication user, including email and display name).
  • Your synced transactions, categories, budgets, and wallets.
  • Your saved AI Assistant advice.
  • Your synced preferences (such as currency and language).
  • Your Premium (Lifetime Sync) entitlement and any remaining AI credits. These are account-bound and are forfeited — deleting your account does not refund a purchase, and you would need to buy again to restore them.

What we keep, and why

  • Purchase records — we retain a record of purchases (with your account identifier removed, i.e. anonymized) for fraud prevention and to stop a purchase from being redeemed twice. This retained record no longer identifies you.
  • Data on your device — deleting your account does not erase the copy of your data stored locally on your phone (this is intentional, so you can keep using FastSpend offline). See the next section to remove it.
  • App-store transaction history — your purchase history is held by Google Play, not by us. Manage or request a refund for purchases through Google Play.

Removing the data on your device

On-device data is stored only in a local database on your phone. To remove it, either uninstall FastSpend, or go to Android Settings → Apps → FastSpend → Storage → Clear storage. This deletes the local database and cannot be undone.

Timing and retention

  • In-app deletion takes effect immediately.
  • Email requests are completed within 30 days of verification.
  • Deleted cloud data is removed from our active systems right away; any residual copies in routine backups are purged in the ordinary course of our backup rotation.
  • Anonymized purchase records (described above) are retained indefinitely for fraud prevention.

Questions

For any question about deleting your data, contact tl.tek5392@gmail.com. For more on how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy.
